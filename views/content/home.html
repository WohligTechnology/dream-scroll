    About us

    Dreamz Securities and Broking Services Pvt. Ltd. started its journey by taking a foundation step in Oct 2006 in Business of serving ones need to achieve peace of mind by fulfilling his Future Financial Security by making him invest in Equity Markets, Mutual Fund, Currency Markets and Debt Markets through our systematic investment strategies.

    Research is our solid foundation on which our advisory service is based. Majority of our strength and revenue is invested in hiring and developing team of analyst to be part of our advisory division. At present we have a team of 5 Research Analyst which are on continu/ous basis involved in research of various companies and asset class. Our research team focuses on companies that have the capacity to offer sustainable long-term business growth The team attempts to research and advise the client to invest in diversified stocks across capitalization, sectors and industries. Team conducts fundamental research and seek to identify under-rated companies, perhaps our research tend to have big, medium and smaller sized companies. We believe these businesses are often under researched, under-owned, undervalued and has the capacity to offer outstanding returns
